About Us

Our Story

Bleach Resistant is based in Guelph, Ontario, Canada. Formerly known as FinPrint Inc., we were founded on a passion for creating high-quality custom sublimated apparel that stands up to the toughest conditions.

What started as a small printing operation has grown into a full-service custom printing company. We specialize in dye sublimation printing on premium polyester fabrics, delivering vibrant, long-lasting prints for teams, businesses, and individuals across Canada and beyond.

Every product we make is crafted with care and attention to detail. From design to delivery, we ensure that each piece meets our high standards of quality and durability.
