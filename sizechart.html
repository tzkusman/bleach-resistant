Size Chart

How to Measure

To find your perfect fit, use a flexible tape measure and wear fitted clothing. Take measurements snugly but not too tight, and keep the tape level around your body for the most accurate results.

  • Chest — Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level under your arms.
  • Waist — Measure around your natural waistline, at the narrowest point of your torso.
  • Length — Measure from the top of your shoulder down to the desired hem length.
